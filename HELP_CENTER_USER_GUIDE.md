## How to Use

### Opening Help Center
1. Look for the **?** (Help icon) in the top toolbar
2. Click the icon
3. Help Center modal opens with "Getting Started" section displayed

### Navigating Sections
- **Sidebar (Left)**: Lists all 6 help sections
- **Click any section**: Content on the right changes instantly
- **Each section has**: Title, organized paragraphs, bullet points, and explanations

### Searching Help
- **Search Box** (at top of sidebar): Ready for future search functionality
- **Currently**: Shows all sections (can be filtered later)

### Closing Help Center
- **Click X button**: Upper right corner
- **Press ESC key**: Keyboard shortcut to close
- **Any method**: Modal closes smoothly
